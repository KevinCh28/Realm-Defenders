Realm Defenders

Instructions

1) Monsters appears from the right side of the map

2) Game over when monster reaches left side of the map

3) Place defender unit by clicking on the map

4) Select defender by clicking on selectable units from top left corner

5) Defender will throw weapons to kill monsters in their row

*Hint* Place defenders far left on same row as an monster

Units

1) Defender #1 is selected by default at the start of the game

2) Switch between defenders to summon by clicking on them at the top left corner of the game

#1 Resource cost = 100

#2 Resource cost = 300
